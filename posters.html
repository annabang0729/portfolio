A STREET CAR NAMED DESIRE
Designed as a poster for a famous theatre show, A Street Car Named Desire. The poster conveys the ill-mindedness of the main character Blanche who eventually loses her sanity. "I don't want realism. I want magic." is a direct quote from the play that clearly demonstrates Blanche's tendency to escape reality by masking her true self through deception and manipulation.
