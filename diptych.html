THEIR EYES WERE WATCHING GOD
Using two contrasting quotes from the novel "Their Eyes Were Watching God" by Zora Neale Hurston, I created a set of posters that represents the progression of the life of Janie, the protagonist of the story.The first poster depicts the moment when Janie is suddenly aware of the blooming nature surrounding her, also metaphorical of her sexual desires. The second poster depicts Janie's despair and sorrow that hits her after the death of her lover, Tea Cake.
